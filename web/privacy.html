Draft Pre-Launch

Privacy Notice

This draft explains how Nivvi handles data during pre-launch. It will be expanded before public release.

Data We Collect

For waitlist and product onboarding, we may collect identity, contact details, account metadata, and usage events required to operate the service.

Communication Consent

Waitlist updates are sent only to users who provide explicit consent. You can opt out at any time via support.

Security Posture

Nivvi is built around approval controls, policy checks, and immutable audit logs. Security controls are continuously reviewed while infrastructure matures.

Data Requests and Deletion

Email privacy@nivvi.io for access, correction, or deletion requests.
